Salsa verde para chilaquiles

Ingredientes

  • -1.5kg of green tomatoes
  • -2 onions
  • -1/2 Garlic
  • -A sprig of cilantro
  • -3 spoons of salt
  • -12 chilitos serranos

Pasos

  1. -Divide the ingredients in equal portions, turn on a pot with some oil inside (4 spoons) and let it warm
  2. -Blend the ingredients and put every portion inside the pot, let it cook
  3. -Wait till it boils, then you can pur it over some deep fried tortilla with some grilled cheese and sour cream
  4. Enjoy!
