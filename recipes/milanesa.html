Milanesa de pollo

Ingredients

  • -1.5kg Flattened breast
  • -Onion powder
  • -Pepper
  • -Salt
  • -Breadcrumbs
  • -One cup of milk
  • -1 egg

Steps

  1. -Add the cup of milk with the onion powder, the egg, the pepper and some soy sauce
  2. -Once it is integrated put the chicken breast to soak in it for 15 minutes
  3. -After the 15 minutes take a bowl, put the breadcrumbs inside and bread the chicken
  4. -Put a pan on the stove with oil, when it is hot cook the chicken on it with salt
  5. -Enjoy
